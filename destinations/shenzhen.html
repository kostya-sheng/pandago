Shenzhen

Silicon Valley of the East

A Modern Metropolis of Tech and Design

Shenzhen is a city that rose from a small fishing village to a global technology hub in just a few decades. Known for its innovation, skyscrapers, and experimental architecture, it's the dynamic heart of the Greater Bay Area.

Whether you're visiting the massive electronics markets of Huaqiangbei, strolling through the OCT-LOFT creative parks, or relaxing on the beaches of Dameisha, Shenzhen offers a glimpse into China's future.

Best Time to Visit Autumn (Oct–Dec)
Must Eat Shenzhen Roasted Goose, Seafood
Innovation Hub Over 10,000 High-Tech Companies
Shenzhen Highlights

Huaqiangbei Electronics Market

The world's largest electronics market, where you can find everything from microchips to the latest tech gadgets in massive, multi-story buildings.

OCT-LOFT Creative Culture Park

A former industrial area transformed into a hub for designers, artists, and musicians, featuring galleries, boutiques, and trendy cafes.

Ping An Finance Centre

One of the tallest buildings in the world, offering an observation deck with breathtaking views of the city's skyline and the border with Hong Kong.
